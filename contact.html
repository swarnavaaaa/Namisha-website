Professional Networking & Guidance

If you would like to connect for professional networking, referrals, academic or clinical discussion, guidance, or exchange of ideas related to psychotherapy and psychoanalytic theory, you are welcome to reach out. This space is open to students, mental health professionals, and colleagues who may wish to engage in thoughtful dialogue, seek perspectives, or learn more about my clinical approach outside of active psychotherapy.

You may reach out via the email address provided on this website. I do not charge for these professional interactions and will respond as promptly as my clinical commitments allow.
